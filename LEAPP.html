LEAPP: Lower-Limb Extendable and Affordable Pediatric Pylon

For my biomedical engineering senior design project, my team and I designed, manufactured, and tested an adjustable prosthetic pylon for pediatric applications in developing countries. We produced a device conforming to the strength requirements outlined in the ISO 10328:2016 standard and were awarded first prize in the design competition. Watch our video below!
